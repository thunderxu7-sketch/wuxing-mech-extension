Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. What data we collect

WuXing Calibrate ("the Extension") collects only the birth date and time you voluntarily enter. This information is used solely to calculate your daily Five Elements (WuXing) energy calibration.

2. How data is stored

All data is stored locally on your device using the Chrome storage.local API. Your birth date, daily calibration cache, language preference, and share settings never leave your browser unless you explicitly choose to share them.

3. Analytics

The Extension may collect anonymous, aggregated usage events (e.g., feature usage counts) to help improve the product. These events contain no personally identifiable information. You can opt out of analytics at any time through the Extension settings.

4. Third-party services

The Extension contains affiliate links to third-party e-commerce platforms. When you click these links, you leave the Extension and are subject to those platforms' own privacy policies. We do not share any of your data with these platforms.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or disclose your personal data to any third party.

6. Data deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser's local storage. No server-side data exists to delete.

7. Children's privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at thunderxu7@gmail.com.
